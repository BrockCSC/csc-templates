\usepackage{csc-poster}

\begin{document}

%% TITLE
% This command lays out the top of the document.
% The first parameter is the title.
% The second parameter is the speaker.  This parameter may be empty.
\cschead{Entertainment Night}{Computer Science Club}

%% SUBTITLE
% This command prints the sub-title and is completely optional.
\cscsubtitle{Be Stress Free for Exams!}

This night will be a combination of board games, console gaming, and LAN games.
Exams are close. Enjoy yourself before the end of the term.\\
\\
This is a \textbf{FREE} event and anyone is welcome. The CSC will be open for snacks and drinks as usual.

%% FOOTER
% The first through third arguments tell us the date and time.
% The last argument is a pithy saying.  It may be empty.
% Following the footer, the CSC logo is printed.
\cscfoot{Friday, April 8}{5:30 PM}{Computer Science Department (J-block)}{}
