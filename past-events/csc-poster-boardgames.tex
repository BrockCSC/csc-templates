\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{csc-poster}

\begin{document}

%% TITLE
% This command lays out the top of the document.
% The first parameter is the title.
% The second parameter is the speaker.  This parameter may be empty.
\cschead{Board Game Night}{Computer Science Club}

%% SUBTITLE
% This command prints the sub-title and is completely optional.
\cscsubtitle{You won't be board!}

Join us this Friday evening for board games! Popular games at past events were \emph{Monopoly, Settlers of Catan, Risk, Chess, Ricochet Robots} and many more. Of course you can also bring your own board game(s).\\

This is a \textbf{FREE} event and everyone is welcome. The CSC will be open for snacks and drinks as usual.

%% FOOTER
% The first through third arguments tell us the date and time.
% The last argument is a pithy saying.  It may be empty.
% Following the footer, the CSC logo is printed.
\cscfoot{Friday, October 1}{5:00 PM}{MCJ328 (Breakout Room)}{See you there!}

\end{document}
